Support KoboToolbox

Here at KoboToolbox we believe in inclusive and accessible data collection for all, so we work hard to keep our tool free and open source. Data collection can empower communities around the world to tell their stories, share their experiences and receive the support they need to overcome adversity. With your support, we can keep KoboToolbox free and open to everyone, but also ensure it remains the innovative resource that has helped so many around the world.
